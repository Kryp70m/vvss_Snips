How To Use
1. Fresh Alerts
Watch the top table first. New qualified events appear there with side, size, age, target, and score.
2. Saved Alerts
Every alert is saved automatically. Use this section to review entry, stop, target, current price, and status.
3. Entry Plan
Open the entry plan only after an alert. Use it as a decision checklist, not as an automatic trade command.
4. Chart Button
Click the chart link beside a name to check the live market before taking any action.
5. Target Filter
Set your preferred move size. Higher targets usually mean fewer alerts and stricter filtering.
6. Coin File
Upload your private list when you want the platform to watch only your selected names. The names are hidden in the settings area.
7. Exchange Lists
Use Auto Load to build a refined list from exchange data, or upload a private list for any venue. The platform shows only count and alert results.
8. Telegram
Add bot token and chat ID to receive alert notifications outside the browser.
9. Risk Disclaimer
Your own analysis and risk manager are important. Do not trade blindly. Trading contains risk, alerts can fail, and losses are possible.
